Common Downgrade Patterns

Three-Dimension Incomplete

Most papers only cover Usability, neglecting the Transparency or Persuasive Design / Dark Patterns dimensions.

Non-Core Focus

General reviews or background literature without interface / HCI depth analysis; cannot directly support the core research question.

Non-Empirical / Tool Discussion

Industry tool discussions or attitude explorations without empirical HCI evidence or interface design analysis (e.g., Friction Mapping).

Tier Rating System

Tier 2 papers serve as supplements; Tier 1 is reserved for core theoretical / empirical pillars (e.g., Gray 2018); Tier 1 Highest for chain-critical papers (e.g., Seamful XAI, Contestable XAI).
